About Me:

Hello world! I'm Joey Treul. Although from Anaheim, CA, I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Chemical Engineering at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities, and have stayed here to pursue a PharmD. I have a great interest in pediatrics because I love the challenges and intricacies that goes into pediatric pharmacy across the broad spectrum of kids' ages.
I'm seeking to pursue a PGY1 that aligns with my interests in pediatrics. My interests lies in pediatric and neonatal critical care, emergency medicine, and informatics. I love problem solving and critical thinking skills needed for these complex and time-sensitive cases.
Outside of the world of pharmacy, I love traveling, online gaming, spending time with family and friends, and playing volleyball (which is currently a work in progress).
